TEAM IITK MOTORSPORTS is a student-run team at one of the premier technical institutes of the country, Indian Institute of Technology, Kanpur. We are a team of highly motivated, enthusiastic, passionate and dedicated students, comprising from a spectrum of departments, with a strong urge to complement the theoretical knowledge imparted in classrooms, and bring them to life, emphasizing on the practical aspects of engineering. Basically, a team building new and advanced prototypes and designs of "Racecar Vehicles".
